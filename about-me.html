About Me

Anai Case

My name is Anai Case, and I am a Jamaican American Artist born and raised in Miami, Florida. Throughout my lifetime, I pursued multiple forms of art, including dance, graphic design, creative writing, and visual arts. You could say I knew I loved art from a young age. My goal is to become a published author and a concept artist. I love making up stories and character designs the most when it comes to art. Although those are not all, my mind is limited too. I am open to other forms of art as I continue to learn and grow as an artist.
