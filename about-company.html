Anonymous Design

Anonymous Design is the parent company that specializes in the arts, including graphics, animations, and other related fields. The Company consists of other subdivisions within. Currently, there are two official branches: Anonymous Motion and Anonymous Ink. Anonymous Motion’s focus is on animation, Encompassing Animated shorts and logos. Anonymous Ink's focus is on still works, both digitalized and traditional works. In the future, I plan to expand Anonymous Design further with other subdivisions.
